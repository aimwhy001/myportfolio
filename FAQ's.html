Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the student loan and repayment.

Under what circumstances would NELFUND deny an application?

if such an applicant is: Proven to have defaulted in respect of any previous
loan granted by any license financial institution; Found guilty of submitting
fake/fraudulent documents and dismissed for exam malpractices by any
school authority; and Convicted of fraud and forgery, drug offences, cultism,
felony, or any offences involving dishonesty;

Are there any documents to upload during the application process?

Yes. Scanned admission letter for new students
(compulsory), scanned Student Identification Card (optional).
